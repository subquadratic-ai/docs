---
title: Numerals
description: Convert spoken numbers to digits with numerals=true
---

The numerals feature converts spoken numbers into their digit form in the transcript.

## Usage

Add `numerals=true` as a query parameter:

```bash
curl -X POST "https://api.aldea.ai/v1/listen?numerals=true" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_ALDEA_API_KEY" \
  --data-binary @audio.wav
```

## Examples

| Spoken | Without `numerals` | With `numerals=true` |
|---|---|---|
| "twenty three" | twenty three | 23 |
| "one hundred and fifty" | one hundred and fifty | 150 |
| "two thousand twenty six" | two thousand twenty six | 2026 |

For broader formatting that also covers punctuation and capitalization, use [smart formatting](/docs/concepts/transcript-formatting/smart-formatting) instead.
